In Pursuit Of

Simplicity

“It is always the simple that produce the marvelous” - Amelia Barr

I find that people equate simplicity with less work. When actually simplicity can be more work than complex. Simplicity, to me, is a practice of decreasing the inessentials to master the essentials.

Quality

“Quality is remembered far after price is forgotten” - Aldo Gucci

Quality is where trust is built and loyalty is earned. If you’re doing something and not giving it your best, it’s not worth doing.

Efficiency

“If I had six hours to chop down a tree, I’d spend the first four sharpening the axe.”
- Abraham Lincoln

Efficiency (for myself and users) is my goal and the only way to achieve it is by spending time effectively.

What I Value

Empathy

UX/UI Design is all about designing with the user in mind. The only way to do that well is with a strong foundation of empathy to work with. I’ve been working out mine for years between my personal and professional life.

Reliability

I have a solid track record of being reliable and loyal to my commitments. I’m a firm believer that the level of reliability you perform at is directly related to your true core values.

Design Thinking

Before I could ever put it into words, I had an eye for what looked good and what worked. Now, having a formal training in design, I’ve learned to hone in on what about a design isn’t working and what it takes to allow elements to come together and compose a beautiful design.
